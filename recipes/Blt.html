BLT

Description

This is a classic. One of my daughters favorite sandwiches. Even if you are a picky eater you will love this sandwich. The best part is, you can customize it anyway you want. I love adding avocado to mine.

Pro tip - I like using pre cooked bacon from costco its much faster!

Ingredients

  • Bread - You can use any type of bread. I like normal white bread.
  • Bacon - You can use any type of bacon. Pre cooked is much faster.
  • Tomato - Greenhouse tomatoes work just fine. Make sure you grab a firm ripe tomato.
  • Lettuce - Grab a head of lettuce from your supermarket. Doesn't need to be special.
  • Mayo - Helmans is the best. Restaurant quality mayo. Or you can make your own its easy!

Directions

Approx 15 min with uncooked bacon. 3 minutes with pre cooked

  1. Cook your bacon or if you have pre cooked bacon wait
  2. Cut your tomato into thin pieces
  3. Break apart your lettuse. I like to smash the base it makes it easier to pull apart
  4. Put lettuce and tomato on a plate set aside
  5. Stick your bread in the toaster. Time will vary depending on how dark you want the bread. I toast mine for 2 minutes
  6. Once bread has been toasted add your condiments
  7. Add your lettuce tomato and bacon then you're finished
  8. I like to add a slice of cheese , ham and avocado to mine. You can customize yours however you want too!
  9. Eat and enjoy!
