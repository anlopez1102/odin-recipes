Lasagna

Description

This mouthwatering lasagna recipe will have your family BEGGING for more. All you need is a few ingredients, a 8x12" pan, and a spatula. Homemade lasagna is one of my favorite meals. This wont dissapoint.

Tip use oven bake noodles so you don't have to boil them

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles- You can use regular lasagna noodles but I would use oven bake noodles
  • Ground Beef- 80/20 ground beef is best. You don't want your ground beef to be too lean. Fat equals flavor. If you would like to go vegetarian mushrooms is a great substitute.
  • Garlic- Freshly minced garlic will add more flavor. If all you have is garlic powder feel free to use that in its place. We prefer minced garlic for this recipe.
  • Sphagetti Sauce- Use your favorite jar of spaghetti sauce. Our preferred sauce is Rao's marinara. Its not exaclty sphagetti sauce but it is delicios.
  • Parmesean- For the freshest taste you should shred your own lasagna. However you can Use the powdered kind
  • Mozzerella- In my recipe I use whole milk mozzerella. It melts perfectly and has a rich flavor. Mozzerella is a must. If you have to substitue provolone is ok
  • Cottage Cheese- 4% and whole milk cottage cheese has a better flavor and texture. Our preferred brand is horizon organic

Instructions

  1. Grease 8x12 pan with avocado oil or olive oil
  2. Brown ground beef in a large skillet
  3. Drain grease
  4. Turn heat to medium and add 2 tbps of garlic cook for another 2 minutes
  5. Add 1 jar of sphagetti sauce to beef stir and cook for 3 minutes
  6. Add 16oz of cottage cheese (should be the whole jar)
  7. Cook for another 3-5 minutes then set aside
  8. In your 8x12 pan spread the sauce on the bottom
  9. You are going to alternate sauce and 3 noodles when you have a layer of sauce layer 3 noodles then add more sauce
  10. Make sure the top layer is sauce not bare noodles
  11. Sprinkle 1 cup of mozzerella on top
  12. IF YOU USED OVEN BAKE NOODLES add 1/4 cup of water to the noodles otherwise they will be dry
  13. Cover with aluminum foil then cook for 30 minutes
  14. Uncover then add 1 1/2 cups of mozzerella cheese then bake for another 5
  15. Let it rest for 10 minutes then enjoy!
