Chicken Alfredo

Description

This is my wifes favorite recipe. Once you get it down, this recipe is very easy to make. Most of the time I don't measure ingredients. If you are a fan of very creamy alfredo then this is the alfredo for you. I like to top it with chicken. However you can do crab, chicken, shrimp etc.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs - 1-2Lbs I like chicken thighs better than breast. Chicken thighs are less likely to get dry.
  • Heavy Whipping Cream - 2 cups Use any brand. I buy the cheap kind from meijer. Doesn't need to be name brand.
  • Mozzerella Cheese - 16 oz Whole milk mozzerella cheese is the goto. It has a richer flavor and thickens the sauce well
  • Parmesean Cheese - 1 cup Freshly grated parmesean is the best. However I use the kind off of the shelf its still delicious
  • Fettuchini Noodles - 1/2 Box. Grab whatever brand you like. We use barilla brand non gluten fettuchini noodles.
  • Butter - 2 Tbps I use grass fed butter from costco. You can use whatever you like.
  • Avocado Oil - 2 Tbps A lot of people use olive oil. I use avocado oil because its healthy and doesn't smoke.
  • Garlic - 2-3 Tbps I like a lot of garlic so I add extra. Use minced garlic.
  • White Pepper - 1/2 Tsp Adds a little flavor and doesnt change the color of the alfredo
  • Lemon - 1/2 a lemon to maranade the chicken with.
  • Paprika - 1 Tbps This is part of the chicken marinade adds color and flavor
  • Salt - To taste. Add as little or as much salt as you would like. I normally don't add it to the alfredo but I add it to the chicken.
  • Black Pepper - To taste. Added to the marinade.
  • Onion Powder 1 Tbsp. This adds really good flavor to the chicken.

Instructions

Takes approx 1 hour 30 minutes

  1. Add 1 tbsp of avocado oil to a ziplock bag
  2. Add chicken thighs, paprica, lemon, salt, black pepper and onion powder to ziplock. Shake well let marinade sit in fridge for 1 hour
  3. After chicken has been marinading for 45 minutes prep your grill
  4. Grab a beer to drink
  5. Once grill is prepped grill chicken to 165 degrees
  6. WHen cooked put chicken on a plate or in a tray then cover will foil. Set aside.
  7. In a big sauce pan add butter and avocado oil
  8. In a tall pot start boiling water
  9. Once butter is fully melted add your minced garlic and white pepper
  10. Once garlic starts to brown and gives off that great aroma dump in heavy whipping cream. Turn your heat to high
  11. Add fettuchini noodles to the boiling water cook to instructions on box
  12. Stir often once the heavy whipping cream starts to boil/froth. Turn heat to medium low
  13. Stir in your parmesean cheese. It will clump use a non abrasive wisk
  14. Once your parmesean cheese is fully incorporated into the heavy cream add your mozzerella and stir
  15. Once the mozzerella cheese is melted set large pan aside
  16. Strain your noodles
  17. Cut chicken into strips. On a plate add noodles and sauce then top with chicken
  18. Enjoy the best alfredo you will ever have!
